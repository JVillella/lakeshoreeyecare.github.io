Dr. Shaheen Gillani

Dr. Shaheen Gillani graduated with Honors in 2006 from the University of Waterloo, receiving both her Bachelor degree of Science and Doctorate of Optometry. Throughout her educational career she received numerous awards including the Prize for Academic Excellence in Ocular Pharmacology, the Essilor Award, the Alcon continuing professional education award, and the Advanced Medical Optics Prize.

Dr. Gillani entered into private practice after graduation. Her areas of interest include pediatrics, contact lens, and cornea. She also co-manages in surgeries including cataract and LASIK. She spent two years living in Austin, Texas, where she had her own private practice and gained a wealth of clinical experience in eye disease management.

Now settled in the beautiful town of Oakville, Dr. Gillani enjoys many of the local activities while spending time with her husband and daughter. She is also an active member of the Ontario Association of Optometrists, the Canadian Association of Optometrists, and the Canadian Coalition of Eye Care Professionals. Dr. Gillani thoroughly enjoys getting to know her patients and their families, and looks forward to meeting new patients.
